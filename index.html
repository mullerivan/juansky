Resume

  • Brief Resume

    • Juan Bergada

      I was born at the Patagonian mountains of San Martin de los Andes in Argentina, and have been skiing since I have memory. The Arlberg was my place, where I learned to love the snow and mountains. With Alpine racing and riding off-piste back home and in Austria I developed skills and techniques that were a strong foundation to my current performance as a freeskier. When I came back to Austria after 9 years of living in Miami , I instantly realized that this is the career and sport I wanted to establish as part of my life. Training continues all year for me, when winter ends in Europe and Argentina, I go for skateboarding,sport climbing, cliff diving, hiking . Eating healthy and training all year long is the key on keeping my mind and body balanced.
